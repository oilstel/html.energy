diy html energy shirt

Last year at the Rotterdam freewrite, our friend Hunter made screen printed bootlegged HTML Energy shirts. It felt like the perfect activity to pair with writing time. We wanted to open source the design, so you can make your own HTML Energy shirt too!
🎨 What You'll Need:
  • Old blank t-shirts (thrifted works best)
  • Lime green screen printing ink
  • Squeegee
  • A local screen printer to burn your screen
⭐ Design Files:
  • Design width: 8.5 inches (21.6 cm)
  • Download front design (star + text)
  • Download back design
📝 Steps:
  1. Find a local screen printer and bring them the HTML Energy design
  2. Have them burn a screen for you (usually takes 1-2 days)
  3. Get your blank shirts ready
  4. Print at home using your screen, ink, and squeegee!
