Happy July! We wanted to send a .html to the wwworld to let you know that we're still here and that we're still writing HTML.

We also wanted to let you know that HTML Day (an annual celebration of HTML) is just around the corner.

This year (2025), it's happening on Saturday, August 2, 2025 around the world!

Last year (2024), 22 cities hosted their own HTML Day. It happened in: Sydney, New York City, Rotterdam, Seoul, Shanghai, Toronto, Honolulu, San Francisco, Vancouver, Montreal, Stockholm, Hamburg, London, Saskatoon, Buenos Aires, Dublin, Austin, Portland (Maine), Atlanta, Lee/Lenox, and Seattle. There are some photos/videos here and here and here!

This year (2025), we have a few confirmed cities, like Sydney, Toronto, etc. Want to be a part? Fire up your local event .html website for Saturday, August 2nd. Specific instructions over at our events.html page!

We organized last year's gatherings with the help of our dedicated community (3,000+ people), all for free. This year, our goal is to make this initiative more sustainable so we can support both our local organizers and ourselves.

We’re reaching out to individuals and companies who might be able to help us make HTML Day possible this year. It's as simple as becoming an angel through our website or subscribing to our Patreon.

If you're interested in helping us make HTML Day possible this year, please email us at sponsor@html.energy.

Thank you for your support!
