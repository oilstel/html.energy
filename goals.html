HTML Energy has 3 goals right now:
  1. The human goal: to show people that the web is still alive. And to actively make the web more human and handmade.
  2. The educational goal: to teach people of all ages to write HTML. Our dream would be to get HTML into schools or curriculums.
  3. The energetic goal: to reveal the raw energy of HTML. Approaching HTML as you would anything that excites you in life.
Right now, these goals are expressed as our podcast (that celebrates practitioners & fans of HTML), our freewrites (that invite people to learn & write HTML), and our general excitement about HTML.
